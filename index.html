Biography

Robert L. Schwartz is a professional investor in public credit markets, responsible for co-managing over $3.5bn in fixed income assets. Robert specializes in portfolio construction across various fixed income sectors including investment-grade corporates, high-yield corporates, mortgage-backed securities, international and emerging market debt, as well as government and agency bonds. Robert is also an active private angel investor, focusing on early-stage technology and healthcare companies that are working to disrupt traditional ecosystems. Specifically, pre-seed, seed, and Series A companies with a scalable business model and a large addressable market. Prior to public credit markets, Robert worked in private wealth management at First Commonwealth Bank and corporate finance at The PNC Financial Services Group. Robert earned his MBA from Carnegie Mellon University and his Bachelor's of Science in Finance and Legal Studies from the Indiana University of Pennsylvania. He is a registered Investment Adviser holding his FINRA Series 65 license (CRD#: 7553616). When time allows, Robert enjoys traveling, cooking, and the cinema.
