Welcome


Hello! My name is Robert Schwartz. I'm currently an MBA student at the Carnegie Mellon University, Tepper School of Business. I'm also an Associate Portfolio Manager at Federated Hermes.

Work Life


As a portfolio manager in the fixed income world, I co-manage ~$2 billion in assets across 10+ separately managed account ("SMA") strategies. These strategies are used by financial advisers across the U.S. to fulfill taxable fixed income mandates for their clients. My professional expertise lies in investment-grade corporate bonds, U.S. government bonds, and agency bonds. However, I do have proficiency in managing portfolio exposures to HY corporate bonds, international bonds, MBS, and ABS.

Academic Life


My undergraduate studies took place at the Indiana University of PA, where I earned my B.S. in Finance and Legal Studies. I also took courses focused on applied statistics and econometrics. Finding enjoyment in the application of statistics to economic and finance topics, I coauthored my first paper, which was presented at the American Economic Association conference hosted by the Federal Reserve Bank of Philadelphia. Subsequently, I went on to coauthor two publications researching the performance of hedge funds. Pursuing my graduate degree at Carnegie Mellon, I intend to expand my understanding of macroeconomics, corporate finance, and data science.
