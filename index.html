Biography

Robert L. Schwartz is a professional investor entrusted with co-managing $4.5bn in fixed income assets. Robert specializes in portfolio construction across various credit sectors including government bonds, agency bonds, investment-grade corporates, mortgage-backed securities, high-yield corporates, and international and EMD. Prior to public credit, Robert worked in private wealth management at First Commonwealth Bank and in corporate finance at The PNC Financial Services Group. In his free time, Robert enjoys being an active angel investor, focusing on early-stage high-growth companies that have the potential to benefit society. Specifically, pre-seed, seed, and Series A companies within technology and healthcare. Robert earned his MBA from Carnegie Mellon University and his Bachelor's of Science in Finance and Legal Studies from the Indiana University of Pennsylvania. He is a registered Investment Adviser Representative holding his FINRA Series 65 license. When time allows, Robert enjoys traveling, cooking, and the movies.
