About Me

Robert L Schwartz is a professional investor in public credit markets, responsible for co-managing over $3bn in fixed income assets. He is also an active private angel investor, focusing on early-stage technology companies that are working to disrupt traditional ecosystems. Prior to working in public credit markets, Robert worked in private wealth management at First Commonwealth Bank and financial planning and analysis at The PNC Financial Group. Robert earned his MBA from Carnegie Mellon University and his B.S. in Finance from the Indiana University of Pennsylvania.
